Executive Summary
Most golfers practise. Almost none of them improve. downwind changes that.
downwind is a voice-first AI golf coaching app that acts as a personal caddy during driving range sessions and simulator rounds. Using your phone and your voice, it listens to your shot feedback after every ball, builds a profile of your game over time, and delivers one precise coaching cue — exactly like a world-class caddy would between shots.

The insight that separates downwind from every generic tip app: it knows the difference between what you think is wrong and what actually is. Most golfers misdiagnose their own swing. downwind tracks patterns across sessions and gently corrects them — without ever echoing back the wrong answer.
Whisper
Your AI caddy. One cue at a time. Calm, precise, never generic. Remembers every session.
Club Analytics
Miss tendencies by club with percentages. Whisper gives you one cue per club to carry into every session.
Programmes
Structured range sessions with a clear objective. No more hitting balls aimlessly. Every session has a purpose.
Course Whisperer
Pre-shot caddy advice for simulator rounds. Set the hole, the pin, the hazard — Whisper tells you exactly what to hit.
Shot Guide
All 22 miss types with visual ball-flight tracers. Golfers learn to name their miss accurately — better data, better coaching.
My Golf
Your profile — handicap, goals, flexibility, injuries. Whisper uses this context in every single coaching response.
Target Market
Mid to high handicap golfers (12–36) who practise at driving ranges and golf simulators without regular access to a teaching pro. Motivated to improve. Smartphone native.
Market Timing
Golf participation surged post-2020 and hasn't retreated. Indoor simulator venues are opening globally at pace. The golfer who plays TrackMan at 9pm on a Tuesday is downwind's primary user — and there are millions of them.
The Opportunity
No app exists that knows your swing history, corrects your self-diagnosis, and gets smarter every session. downwind is that product. Subscription model — Par at $6.99, Birdie at $14.99 — scales cleanly with user growth.
"The worse the golfer, the faster downwind becomes indispensable. High-frequency misses generate the data the model needs — and the more it knows, the better it coaches."

Shot Guide
Know your miss. Name it accurately.
All
Ball Flight
Contact
Trajectory
Slice
Most Common
Ball curves hard left to right. Starts near target then bends away. Most common miss for high handicappers.
"Starts OK then bends right into the trees."
Hook
Common
Ball curves hard right to left. Dives left of target. Caused by closed clubface or excess hand rotation.
"Starts right then dives hard left. Fast and low."
Snap Hook
Severe
Extreme sudden hook. Ball goes dead left instantly with almost no forward movement.
"Violent. Gone left before you finish the swing."
Push
Manageable
Ball goes straight right of target with no curve. Swing path aimed right — different to a slice.
"Solid contact. Just starts and stays right."
Pull
Manageable
Ball goes straight left of target with no curve. Opposite of a push. Swing path aimed left.
"Solid but misses left. No curve at all."
Draw
Controlled
Gentle right-to-left curve. Not a miss — intentional and ideal. Adds distance and penetrating flight.
"Starts right, bends back to target. Satisfying."
Fade
Controlled
Gentle left-to-right curve. Controlled and often intentional. Lands softly. Opposite of draw.
"Starts left, drifts right. High and soft landing."
Chunk
Common
Club hits ground before ball. Divot is behind ball. Shot falls dramatically short and feels heavy.
"Thud. Ground first. Ball dribbles forward."
Thin
Common
Club catches top half of ball. Low, fast flight. Runs well past target. Common on approach shots.
"Rockets low and hot. Runs way through the green."
Shank
Severe
Hosel strikes ball. Shoots violently 45 degrees right. The most dreaded miss in golf.
"That hollow clunk. Ball shoots sideways. Everyone saw it."

Roadmap
What gets built, in what order
1
Now — MVP
Core Shot Loop
Voice input, Whisper coaching response, press-to-talk, voice readback, Reply and Next Shot flow. Running as a mobile web app tested at GolfSpace simulator by the founder. Coaching system prompt tuned for one cue at a time, caddy tone, no echoing the golfer's diagnosis.
2
Next
Database & Profile
Railway Postgres database. Shot history saved across sessions. My Golf profile page — mandatory and optional fields. Whisper uses profile context in every coaching call. Session history page with post-session summaries. Club analytics — tendencies and cues per club.
3
Phase 3
Programmes & Shot Guide
Pre-built range programmes — The Surgeon, The Wedge Whisperer, Off The Tee, The Short Game Lab. Shot Guide with all 22 miss types and visual tracers. Programme selection at session start. Post-session summary tied to programme goal.
4
Phase 4
Monetisation & App Store
Par and Birdie tier paywalls. Stripe payment integration. User accounts and authentication. App Store submission — iOS native app. Par at $6.99/month, Birdie at $14.99/month. Free trial TBD.
5
Phase 5 — Birdie
Course Whisperer & Advanced Coaching
Course Whisperer — pre-shot caddy feature for simulator rounds. Sentiment analysis — Whisper adapts tone based on frustration or confidence. Visual coaching aids — instructional diagrams per swing cue. Mastery progression — 5 levels per skill, Birdie tier only.
6
Phase 6 — Eagle
TrackMan Integration & Video
TrackMan and launch monitor data integration — real ball flight replaces self-reporting. Video swing analysis. ElevenLabs natural voice. Advanced cross-session analytics. Eagle tier launch.
